Clear Creek
Walking Tour

Explore over 7 miles of riparian forest, wetlands, and salmon-bearing creek winding through the heart of Kitsap County. Tap a stop to learn the history, ecology, and stories along the way.

How to use this tour

  1. Press Start Tour to open the map
  2. Tap any numbered marker to read about that stop
  3. Use My Location (◎) to find yourself on trail
  4. Walk at your own pace — no set order required
