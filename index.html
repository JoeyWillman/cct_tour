Silverdale, Washington

Clear Creek
Walking Tour

The Clear Creek Trail is a valuable community resource that thousands of people use each year to enhance their physical and mental health while connecting with nature. Managed by Great Peninsula Conservancy (GPC) in partnership with Kitsap County, the trail stretches from Silverdale Waterfront Park in the south to the Schold Farm in the north. It follows salmon-bearing Clear Creek as it winds through the downtown areas of Kitsap County’s largest retail center, passing through riparian habitats, wetlands, and remnant forests.

How to use this tour

  1. Press Start Tour to open the map
  2. Tap any numbered marker to read about that stop
  3. Use My Location (◎) to find yourself on trail
  4. Walk at your own pace — no set order required
